This is the public shipping demo for HubieQuest (Dream & Grow MVP) — an early interactive prototype designed to help people turn overwhelming goals into small, achievable daily actions.

What HubieQuest Does

Generates daily quests based on a user’s goal

Creates a Dream Quest to support longer-term progress

Tracks progress, streaks, and momentum

Encourages consistency through structured daily action

Collects early feedback to improve the experience

Vision

HubieQuest is designed to help people turn ambition into action.

The goal is simple: reduce overwhelm, build consistency, and make progress feel clear, visible, and achievable.
